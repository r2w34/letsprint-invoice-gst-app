### Platform-Specific Strategies

#### LinkedIn (B2B Focus)
**Frequency**: 3-4 times per week
**Best Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM and 12-2 PM
**Content Mix**:
- 40% Industry insights and thought leadership
- 30% Company news and achievements
- 20% Employee spotlights and culture
- 10% Engagement questions

**Post Length**: 1,200-1,500 characters optimal
**Hashtags**: 3-5 professional hashtags
**Media**: High-quality business photos, infographics, short videos

#### Twitter/X (News & Quick Updates)
**Frequency**: 1-3 times per day
**Best Times**: 9 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM
**Content Mix**:
- 40% Industry news and quick updates
- 25% Company announcements
- 20% Retweets/engagement with industry voices
- 15% Investor-relevant information

**Post Length**: 150-200 characters (concise)
**Hashtags**: 2-3 relevant hashtags
**Strategy**: Thread important announcements, use polls

#### Facebook (Community Building)
**Frequency**: 4-5 times per week
**Best Times**: Wednesday 11 AM-1 PM, Thursday-Friday 1-4 PM
**Content Mix**:
- 35% Educational content and stories
- 30% Visual content (photos, videos)
- 20% Community engagement
- 15% Company updates

**Post Length**: 40-80 words with compelling visuals
**Media**: Videos perform 135% better than photos
**Strategy**: Go Live quarterly for major announcements

#### Instagram (Visual Storytelling)
**Frequency**: 4-6 posts/week + daily Stories
**Best Times**: Monday 11 AM, Tuesday-Friday 10 AM-3 PM
**Content Mix**:
- 50% Behind-the-scenes and culture
- 25% Product/service showcases
- 15% User-generated content (farmer stories)
- 10% Industry education

**Formats**:
- Feed Posts: High-quality images, carousels
- Stories: Daily updates, polls, Q&As
- Reels: Short educational/entertaining videos
- IGTV: Long-form content (quarterly)

**Strategy**: Strong visual branding, consistent filters/style
