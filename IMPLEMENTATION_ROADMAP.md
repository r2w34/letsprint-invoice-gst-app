## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

### Don't Do These

‚ùå **Panic and Rush**
- Don't make hasty public statements without legal review
- Don't engage in arguments with fake accounts
- Don't threaten action you can't follow through on

‚ùå **Inconsistency**
- Don't post sporadically on social media
- Don't ignore small fake accounts (they grow)
- Don't abandon monitoring after initial crisis

‚ùå **Overreaction**
- Don't sue everyone (pick battles wisely)
- Don't be overly defensive in tone
- Don't spam your audience with alerts

‚ùå **Underinvestment**
- Don't try to do everything manually
- Don't skip the monitoring tools
- Don't neglect ongoing content creation

‚ùå **Poor Communication**
- Don't hide issues from leadership
- Don't delay informing stakeholders
- Don't provide different information through different channels
